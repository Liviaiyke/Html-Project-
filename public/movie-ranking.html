The Best Movies According to Olivia

My top 3 movies of all-time

Bridgerton

During the Regency Era in England, eight close-knit siblings of the powerful Bridgerton family attempts to find love.

Ordinary Angels

Inspired by the incredible true story of a hairdresser who single-handedly rallies an entire community to help a widowed father save the life of his critically ill young daughter.

I Am: Celine Dion

This is a journey inside Celine Dion's life as she reveals her battle with Stiff Person Syndrome.
